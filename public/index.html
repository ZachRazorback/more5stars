Frequently asked questions

How does the routing work exactly?

When a customer scans your QR code, they see a simple star page. Tap 5 stars and they're sent to your Google Business Profile to post publicly. Tap 1–4 stars and they instead get a private feedback form that comes straight to you — so you hear about a problem before it becomes a public review. You can adjust the star threshold per location in your dashboard.

Is review gating against Google's policies?

Selectively routing only your highest raters to Google (and diverting lower ratings to a private form) is a form of "review gating." Google's review policies and the U.S. FTC's 2024 rule on review suppression discourage this practice, and Google can filter or remove reviews if it's detected. More5Stars lets you set the threshold per location — including a compliant mode that invites all customers to review while still capturing private feedback — so you choose how to operate. We don't fabricate reviews, post on customers' behalf, or offer incentives.

How long does setup take?

About 5 minutes. Add a location with its name and Google review URL, generate the QR code, and print it. That's it.

Do you support multiple locations?

Yes. Each location gets its own review page, QR code, Google URL, analytics, and private feedback inbox. Pricing is $247/month per location.

The full product — unlimited scans, QR codes, analytics, private feedback capture, and support. A card is required to start, but you aren't charged until the trial ends.
