# More5Stars — Customer Pitch & Setup

## The Problem

Your business gets reviews everywhere — Google, Facebook, Yelp, Instagram. But **low ratings (1-4 stars) hurt you publicly, while good feedback about problems never reaches you.**

**Result:** Bad reviews damage your reputation, and you never find out what went wrong until it's too late.

## The Solution: More5Stars

**Smart review routing.** When a customer rates you:

- **⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5 stars)** → Sent directly to your Google review page (helps your rating)
- **⭐⭐⭐⭐ - ⭐ (1-4 stars)** → Sent to a private feedback form (only you see it)

**Why this works:**
1. **Fix problems before they become reviews** — You see the feedback and resolve it while the customer is still willing to give you another chance
2. **Protect your public rating** — High ratings go public, low ratings get solved privately
3. **Keep customers happy** — They feel heard without damaging your reputation

## How It Works

### For Customers

1. Customer scans QR code (or clicks link)
2. They tap a rating (1-5 stars)
3. If 5 stars → redirected to Google to leave a public review
4. If 1-4 stars → sees a private feedback form to tell you what went wrong

### For You (Business Owner)

1. Log into your More5Stars dashboard
2. See all private feedback in one inbox
3. View analytics: Which ratings are coming in? How many go to Google?
4. Download QR codes to print for your location
5. Respond to feedback, make improvements, watch your Google rating climb

## Compliance Note

This tool is **compliant with Google and FTC policies** when configured correctly:

- **Compliant mode:** Invite everyone to Google (set threshold to 1), capture feedback privately for all ratings
- **Review gating mode:** Only send 5★ to Google, others to private feedback

You choose which mode fits your business. More5Stars supports both.
